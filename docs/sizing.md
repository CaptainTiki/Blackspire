## Current Standards (Locked)

- **Base Module**: 64 units (2.0m)
- **Subdivision**: 16-unit increments (¼ of 64)
- **Player Capsule**: 1.45m
- **Player Camera**: 1.30m
- **Base Texture Size**: 64×64

These values were tested and adopted after validating player feel in 64-unit rooms.
